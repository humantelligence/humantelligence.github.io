Talent Fit Module

The Humantelligence Platform Version 6.0 focus on the Talent Fit Module. This module has as purpose to support the current recruitment process on the client company.

It puts together the candidates cultural profiles, to match that information with the the company’s culture, the direct manager profile and a occupational profile and then rank the candidates based on the Summarized Match Score.

With Talent Fit, the admin/recruiter will have more information to provide insights on the candidate selection. In addition, Talent Fit optimize the selection process increasing the probability of successfull employee adquisition.

Elements of Talent Fit

Talent Fit put together several Elements and Concepts to support the selection process:

Positions

Positions are the open positions available on the company. Each position is related with a Job Family.

Candidates

Candidates can be Current Employess (already members with Scan Completed) or New Members.

Each candidate have to complete the Scan to be ranked on the selection process. In order to support this, the recruiter can invite candidates to complete the scan sending an email invitation.

Each candidate is esentially a individual user on the HT Platform under the Client’s Features Module Setup. At least, each candidate will have the following functionalities available:

  • Profile Page
  • Network
  • User Settings

The main difference from the Candidate perspective is that the Candidate Profile include the name of the Position is running to and the Four Fit Factors Scores.

Matching and Scoring

The TalentFit match, scores and rank the candidates based on Four Fit Factors as Follows:

Fit Factors

  • Occupation: Humantelligence Ideal Profile Benchmark
  • Manager: Direct Report
  • Group: Group Culture
  • Employee Performance: Employee Incumbent Data

Each Fit Factor is a representation of a cultural profile, the same as each candidate have a cultural profile. Each factor is compared against the candidate profile and based on the similarities, each candidate get points.

After each candidate completes the HT Scan or everytime a current employee is added as a candidate, the system run the calculations and rank the entire candidate list in descent order based on the number of points.

Talent Fit, clasify the candidates in 4 groups based on the matching score as follows:

  •       Very High Similarity (8 - 10 points)
  •       High Similarity (6 - 7 points)
  •       Moderate Similarity (3 - 5 points)
  •       Low Similarity (0 - 2 points)

In order to setup what is the culture profile for the Open Position, Talent Fit use weights over the Four Fit Factors, that means, the recruiter can assign an specific weight to each Factor.

Job Family

One of the more important Fit Factors is the Occupation. Depends on the Job Family related with the Open Position, the recruiter will be able to select an Occupation with represent a cultural profile against the candidate’s cultural profiles will be compared to.

The Job Families available are based on HT Benchmarks gather from Market Studies and Experts Judgement.



Guides

The following guides describes the Talent Fit Module funcionalities through the whole cycle of candidates selection.



Position List

The Position List is the easiest way to have a big picture of the current positions on the system.

This list shows the current state of the positions on the systems, including:

* Code: Position Code.
* Position: Position Name.
* Open Positions: Number of open positions in the system.
* Candidates: Number of candidates related with the position.
* Profiles Completed: Percentage of candidates with profiles completed.
* Group: Group where the candidate need to fit in.
* Manager: Direct Manager for the position.

Add New Position

As a first step, the recruiter needs to create a new Position in the system. Please see the following picture:

The recruiter needs to configure the weightings of the fit factors in order to achieve the goal of match candidate to a specific cultural profile. That means, if the weight of the Direct Manager is higher than the others factors, the Position Cultural Profile will match candidate which are similar to the Direct manager, and in the same way works for the other factors.


The Candidates List

To see the candidate list the Recruiter needs to click on the Position Name at the Position List. That action drive the Recruiter to the Candidate List. This list looks as follows:

In this view, the recruiter can:

  • Send direct email using customizable templates (same as from the user administration)
  • Import users as new candidate
  • Export candidate information (including direct links)
  • Move Candidate to the next step
  • Remove Candidates
  • Place Candidates
  • Edit the Candidate General information
  • Add a Candidate Resume PDF file and LinkedIn profile link

In addition to that, The system list the candidates in descent order based on the Match Score and assign a color related with the 4 ranges of fit:

  •       Very High Similarity (8 - 10 points)
  •       High Similarity (6 - 7 points)
  •       Moderate Similarity (3 - 5 points)
  •       Low Similarity (0 - 2 points)

This list give the Recruiter complete information to support the selection process. In this view, the recruiter can see what is the level of match by each factor and by an overall score. From this list, the recruiter can access the candidate profile to see detailed information about his cultural profile.

Edit Position

A position can be edited at any time, however, the recruiter should note that, any change on the Fit Factor Weights will affect the Match and Score Calculations, and those calculation are fired immediately after an update on the Position.

This can affect the selection of the right candidate. Please be careful.
